Amouage

Home / Works / Shot in Iceland

Description

In this project, I designed a promotional poster highlighting the timeless elegance of the AMOUAGE fragrance brand. The goal was to capture the essence of luxury and sophistication through a clean and impactful visual.

The design is built around a rich, deep red gradient that evokes passion, intensity, and the olfactory richness of the fragrance. The bottle is heroically displayed with focused lighting and a subtle reflection, conveying the product’s prestige and mystery.

Elegant typography: serif fonts used to emphasize the noble character of the brand. Clear visual hierarchy: slogan, brand name, description, and promo offer are all distinctly structured for smooth readability. Depth and realism: tilted bottle with shadows and reflections to create a 3D-like effect. Integrated commercial message: “Up to 30% OFF” prominently displayed to merge style with marketing appeal.
